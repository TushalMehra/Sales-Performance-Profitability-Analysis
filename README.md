<h2>🧠 Project Overview</h2>

<p>
Retail businesses generate massive amounts of sales data every day. Analyzing this data
helps organizations understand revenue drivers, identify profitable products, and optimize
business strategies.
</p>

<p>
In this project, I performed an <b>end-to-end analysis of retail sales performance</b>
using <b>Python (Pandas & Matplotlib)</b> for data exploration and cleaning,
<b>SQL</b> for business-focused analysis, and <b>Power BI</b> to build an
interactive dashboard for visual insights.
</p>

<h3>🎯 Project Objectives</h3>

<ul>
<li>Analyze overall sales performance and profitability</li>
<li>Identify top-performing product categories</li>
<li>Discover regional sales trends</li>
<li>Analyze customer purchasing behavior</li>
<li>Generate insights to improve business decision-making</li>
</ul>
